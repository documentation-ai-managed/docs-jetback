## Overview

JetBack is a mobile app that automatically monitors your booked flights for price drops and secures airline refunds or credits with zero effort. After you forward your booking confirmation once, JetBack handles fare tracking, claim filing, and notifications. You save money effortlessly as airlines fluctuate prices daily.

## How It Works

JetBack integrates seamlessly with your booking process. Simply download the app, forward your flight confirmation email or details, and relax. The app scans airline fares continuously and acts instantly on price drops.

<Expandable title="Detailed Workflow" default-open="false">

1. Book your flight normally.
2. Forward confirmation to JetBack.
3. App monitors fares 24/7.
4. Claims filed automatically.
5. Credits applied or notified.

</Expandable>
