## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A recent flight booking confirmation email from your airline
- An iOS or Android device with internet access
- An email account for forwarding confirmations
</Callout>

JetBack works on both iOS and Android. Follow the platform-specific instructions below to get started quickly.

## Download and Install the App

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">
    Download JetBack from the App Store.

    <Image
      src="https://getjetback.com/images/app-store.png"
      alt="JetBack on the App Store"
      width="200"
      height="60"
    />

    1. Open the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/jetback-flight-price-tracker/id6744909694).
    2. Search for "JetBack".
    3. Tap **Get** to install.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Enable notifications during setup for instant refund alerts.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android" icon="android">
    Get JetBack from Google Play.

    <Image
      src="https://getjetback.com/images/google-play.png"
      alt="JetBack on Google Play"
      width="200"
      height="60"
    />

    1. Open the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.getjetback.android).
    2. Search for "JetBack".
    3. Tap **Install**.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Grant email access permissions to forward booking confirmations seamlessly.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Complete Initial Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account" icon="user">
    Launch the JetBack app and sign up with your email or continue with Google/Apple.

    ```
    Email: your.email@example.com
    Password: Create a strong password
    ```

    Verify your email if prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Forward Booking Confirmation" icon="mail">
    Locate your airline booking confirmation email (e.g., from Delta, United).

    Forward it to JetBack's monitoring address:

    ```
    To: monitor@jetback.com
    Subject: Forward: [Your Booking Confirmation Subject]
    ```

    Include the full email thread for complete flight details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Monitoring" icon="zap">
    Return to the app. JetBack automatically detects your flight and starts monitoring for price drops.

    Complete the initial walkthrough:
    - Review tracked flights
    - Enable push notifications
    - Set refund preferences (credits or cash)

    You're now liveâ€”JetBack handles the rest!
  </Step>
</Steps>
