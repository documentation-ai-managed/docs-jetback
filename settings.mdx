## Overview

Customize JetBack to match your preferences. Adjust notifications for fare drops, update your personal details, manage privacy controls, and handle subscriptions directly from the app. These settings ensure you receive timely alerts without unnecessary interruptions.

<Callout kind="tip">
Review your settings after initial setup or app updates to optimize notifications and privacy based on your travel habits.
</Callout>

## Accessing Settings

Reach JetBack settings quickly from the app menu.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Menu" icon="menu">
        Tap the profile icon in the top-right corner.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Settings" icon="settings">
        Scroll to and tap `Settings` from the menu.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Adjust Options" icon="sliders">
        Browse categories like Notifications and Account.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android" icon="monitor">

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Drawer" icon="menu">
        Swipe from the left edge or tap the hamburger icon.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Settings" icon="settings">
        Tap `Settings` in the navigation drawer.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Customize" icon="sliders">
        Use toggles and inputs for each category.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Notification Preferences

Tailor alerts for fare drops, claim updates, and more. Enable push notifications for instant updates on secured credits.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Alert Types" icon="bell">
    Choose from High, Medium, or Low priority for fare changes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quiet Hours" icon="moon">
    Set times to mute notifications, like overnight.
  </Card>
</Columns>

| Preference       | Description                          | Default |
|------------------|--------------------------------------|---------|
| Fare Drop Alerts | Notify on price reductions `{>10%}` | On     |
| Claim Status     | Updates on refund processing        | On     |
| Sound/Vibration  | Customize alert style               | Default |

<Callout kind="info">
Test notifications in Settings to ensure they work with your device configuration.
</Callout>

## Updating Account Information

Keep your details current for accurate refund claims and communications.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Account" icon="user">
    In Settings, select `Account Information`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Fields" icon="edit-3">
    Update name, email, or phone. Use verified details for airlines.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="save">
    Tap `Save` and confirm via email if prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Privacy and Data Sharing

Control what data JetBack accesses and shares.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Data Collection" default-open="true">
    JetBack collects booking confirmations and flight data to monitor fares. Opt out of analytics sharing in `Privacy > Data Usage`.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Third-Party Sharing">
    No personal data shared without consent. Airlines receive only claim details.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Delete Data">
    Request full data deletion via `Privacy > Account Data > Delete All`.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
