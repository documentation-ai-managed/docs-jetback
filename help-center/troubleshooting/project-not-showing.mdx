---
title: Project Not Showing Up
description: Fix missing JetBack projects.
---

## Symptoms

- Project does not appear on your dashboard
- Unable to access new projects

## Causes

- Not logged in with correct account
- Browser cache issues

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your account">
    Ensure you are logged in to the right JetBack account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the page">
    Press Ctrl+Shift+R (or Cmd+Shift+R) to reload your browser cache.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log out and in">
    Sign out and log back in to JetBack.
  </Step>
</Steps>
