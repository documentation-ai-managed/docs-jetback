## Overview

To begin using JetBack, you need to create a project.

## Steps

1. Log in to your JetBack dashboard.
2. Click "New Project" at the top right.
3. Enter a project name and description.
4. Click "Create Project".

Your new project will appear in your JetBack dashboard. You can now add documentation or invite teammates.
