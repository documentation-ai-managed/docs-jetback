## Add a Project to JetBack

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    In JetBack, select the "Projects" tab from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Project">
    Click the blue "New Project" button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details">
    Enter your project's name and a short description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Press "Create Project". Your project will now be visible in JetBack.
  </Step>
</Steps>
